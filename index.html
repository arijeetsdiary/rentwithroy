FAQ

Questions answered.

Everything you need to know before applying. Still unsure? Reach out directly — Roy responds personally.

Do I need a credit history to apply?
+
No. We do not run credit checks. You just need a valid Australian driver's licence and an active account on at least one delivery platform — UberEats, Deliveroo, or DoorDash.
What can I use the car for?
+
You can use the car for UberEats, Deliveroo, DoorDash, and personal use. It is not approved for Uber or DiDi rideshare — that is, carrying passengers for payment. This is clearly stated in the rental agreement.
What is the minimum rental term?
+
The minimum term is 2 weeks. After that, standard renters continue week-to-week at $159 + GST per week. Monthly commitment renters lock in 4-week blocks at $149 + GST per week.
Is there a bond? How does it work?
+
Yes — a $300 refundable bond is required when you collect the vehicle. It is returned in full when you return the car in its agreed condition. The bond covers any damage beyond normal wear, unpaid rent, or outstanding fines at time of return.
What if the car breaks down?
+
If the breakdown is a natural mechanical fault — not caused by the customer — you will not be charged for the days the car is unavailable. Roadside assist is included. Contact Roy directly and he will resolve it as fast as possible to get you back earning.
How much notice do I need to return the car?
+
We ask for at least 7 days' written notice before returning the vehicle. This gives time to arrange the next renter and process your bond refund quickly. Full terms are in the rental agreement.
Who pays for fuel and tolls?
+
Fuel, tolls, and any fines during your rental period are your responsibility. The weekly payment covers the vehicle, insurance, registration, servicing, and roadside assist only.
Can I apply on a student visa or 485 graduate visa?
+
Yes. Provided you hold a valid Australian driver's licence (or an overseas licence with an official English translation) and are legally permitted to work in Australia, you are welcome to apply. Many of our renters are on temporary visas including student and graduate visas.
Where do I pick up and drop off the car?
+
Pickup and drop-off is in Tarneit, VIC 3029. The exact street address is sent to you once your application is approved.
